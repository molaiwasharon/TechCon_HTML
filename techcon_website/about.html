History of TechCon

Founded in 1995 by a group of tech enthusiasts, TechCon rapidly established itself as a premier platform for innovation and collaboration in the technology industry. The inaugural conference in San Francisco attracted 500 attendees, laying the groundwork for its future expansion. Throughout the late 1990s and early 2000s, TechCon evolved to include seminars, workshops, and product demonstrations, featuring notable speakers such as Bill Gates and Steve Jobs, who helped drive forward-thinking discussions and inspire the tech community

Mission

The mission of TechCon is to foster innovation and collaboration in the tech industry through insightful discussions and networking opportunities.
